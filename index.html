About the company

Founded in June 2018, MI Robotic is a Singapore-headquartered developer of applied AI and robotics technology for a variety of different environments including home, school, healthcare, industrial, F&B, and retail. The sole distributor in Singapore for MI Robots, a China-built brand of robot, MI Robotic, together with its sister teams in Malaysia and India, are providers of AI related customize software development and associated IT services for advanced robotics applications.
